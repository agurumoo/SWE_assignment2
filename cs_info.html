About the Department

At George Mason University's College of Engineering and Computing, we believe that true innovation happens when creativity meets cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to shape future leaders who don’t just adapt to the challenges of tomorrow, but who also dream up solutions that make the world cleaner, safer, and more connected.

What makes GMU truly stand out? It's our forward-thinking approach that mixes top-tier education with real-world impact. We harness the power of Northern Virginia’s innovation ecosystem to provide students with hands-on learning, and our global network opens doors to opportunities around the world. Whether you're exploring the exciting worlds of robotics, big data, or Next-G wireless communication, you'll find yourself at the forefront of technological revolutions. Our diverse and inclusive community is committed to supporting students every step of the way—offering flexible learning paths, state-of-the-art classrooms, and access to groundbreaking research. Ready to lead the future? At GMU, your journey starts here.
